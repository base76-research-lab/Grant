# About Grant

Grant is an AI agent designed to help researchers and research teams move faster from funding discovery to high-quality proposal drafts.

## Mission

Grant reduces administrative friction in the funding process while keeping human judgment in control.

## Capabilities

- Opportunity discovery across grant sources
- Profile-based opportunity ranking
- Proposal draft generation with structured outputs
- Submission preparation artifacts for review

## Principles

- Human-in-the-loop by default
- Transparent selection rationale
- Source-aware discovery with clear provenance
- Practical outputs aligned with real submission workflows

## Positioning

Grant is an AI funding copilot.

It supports researchers through discovery, prioritization, drafting, and preparation, while final submission authority stays with the researcher and institution.
