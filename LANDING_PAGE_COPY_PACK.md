## Value Proposition

Grant turns fragmented funding work into one repeatable flow:

- Discover: Find relevant opportunities from trusted sources
- Prioritize: Rank opportunities by profile fit and strategic relevance
- Draft: Generate structured proposal content
- Prepare: Export submission-ready checklists and metadata

## Benefits

- Save time on repetitive grant administration
- Improve targeting with profile-based matching
- Keep control with human-reviewed outputs
- Standardize proposal preparation quality across teams
